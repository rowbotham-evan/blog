Deriving the GHK Current Equation

February 20, 2026

Starting from constant-field assumptions, flux of ion $X$ can be expressed through electrodiffusion and reduced into a closed form current equation.

The core voltage-dependent ratio appears as $$ I_X \propto \frac{V_m\left([X]_i - [X]_e e^{-zFV_m/RT}\right)}{1 - e^{-zFV_m/RT}} $$ which is the characteristic GHK structure.
