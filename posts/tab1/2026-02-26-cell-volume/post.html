A Mathematical Model of Cell Volume

February 26, 2026

This note studies osmotic balance in a single compartment cell model. A compact starting point is $\frac{dV}{dt} = L_p A (\Pi_i - \Pi_e)$, where $V$ is cell volume and $\Pi$ is osmotic pressure.

Using van't Hoff approximations, one convenient form is $$ \Pi = RT\sum_i c_i $$ so water flow is driven by concentration mismatch.
