Testing Math?

February 26, 2026

Math helps me test ideas with precision instead of relying on vague intuition.

A compact identity I love is: $$ e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0 $$

And for dynamics, I often start from $\frac{dN}{dt} = rN\left(1 - \frac{N}{K}\right)$ to reason about stability and growth limits.
